Human Activity Recognition

Ever wondered if a machine could understand what you're doing just by watching? This intelligent system does exactly that. Powered by deep learning and built with Flask, it analyzes video input to recognize human activities like walking, jumping, sitting, or waving—just like a digital observer with a sharp eye. Whether you're building smart surveillance, fitness apps, or interactive environments, this system turns raw video into real-time insights with ease and accuracy.
